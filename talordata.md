## About the Google Maps SERP API

TalorData's Google Maps SERP API helps developers retrieve structured local search data from Google Maps without running browsers, rotating proxies, or maintaining parsers.

It is useful for:

* Local SEO and rank tracking
* Business lead enrichment
* Store, restaurant, hotel, and service-area research
* Competitor monitoring across cities and countries
* AI agents that need fresh local search context

Instead of handling blocks, layout changes, retries, and localization yourself, you can request Google Maps search data through TalorData and receive normalized results that are easier to store, analyze, and ship into your own product.
